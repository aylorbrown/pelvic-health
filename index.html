ROOT

ROOT is a pelvic floor exercise app for people with vaginas. In eastern traditions, the pelvic floor is associated with the root chakra - it’s where we tend to hold fears around our health, safety, and financial security.

While many women’s health apps define pelvic floor health in terms of a woman using her body in service of others - heterosexual sex or childbirth - ROOT centers the importance of a strong pelvic floor in terms of a woman's mental and physical health.
